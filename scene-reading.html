Size Up the Scene Before You Commit

This module trains you to read a scene and predict where the hazard is going before any action is taken. The goal is not to identify the exact chemical — it is to ask: "Where is this going, and where do I need to be?"

🌬️
Vapor Movement Predict where airborne hazards will travel based on wind and vapor density.
🗺️
Terrain Awareness Identify how slopes, ditches, and confined spaces trap and channel hazards.
📍
Safe Positioning Determine where command, staging, and exposures need to be protected.
🚧
Zone Establishment Apply isolation principles before hazmat arrival.
