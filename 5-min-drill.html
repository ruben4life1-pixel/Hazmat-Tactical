Basic Hazmat Tactical Tool — Free
After completing a Brain Drill, open the Basic Tool to look up the chemical you just trained on. Verify its vapor density (does it sink or rise?), review TWA / STEL / IDLH values, and use the interactive plume diagram with wind slider to visualize downwind protection zones. The ERG isolation distances are built in — reinforce what the scenario taught you with real data for that specific chemical.
Advanced Hazmat Tactical Tool — Paid
Technician-level drills often reference calculations and monitoring decisions. The Advanced Tool puts those calculations in your hands: the LEL-to-PPM Converter shows exactly when a chemical becomes toxic before it becomes explosive; the VOC Correction Factor Calculator covers 85+ chemicals so your PID readings are accurate; the Inverse Square Law Calculator solves radiation stay-time math on the fly; and GEBMO Behavioral Modeling predicts container behavior under stress. Every drill answer that mentions a number — that number is verifiable here.
