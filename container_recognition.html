◆ Core Training Principle
At an operations level, your first job is to recognize the hazard — not mitigate it. Container type gives you a probability range before any placard is legible. An oval-section tanker near a gas station is almost always carrying flammable liquids. A ton container at a water treatment plant is almost certainly chlorine. This module sharpens that instinct. Open every explanation after answering — the design rationale and common mistakes are where the real learning happens.
