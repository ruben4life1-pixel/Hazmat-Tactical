About Us

Hazmat Tactical™ was built by a Chicago firefighter and hazmat technician with a singular mission: to provide highly accessible, fiercely practical training resources for responders.

Whether you're an instructor needing fresh drill ideas, an officer leading tabletop sessions, or a department looking for an affordable solution (especially volunteer squads), this platform is built to help you prepare for the real thing.

Please note: The tools generated on this platform are for training purposes only and must never be utilized to dictate tactics during an active incident.

  • Built by a Firefighter
  • Designed for Real Incidents
  • Saves Time Building Drills
  • Affordable for All Departments
